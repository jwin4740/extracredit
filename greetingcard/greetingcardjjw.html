Create Your Card!!

1) Hover over card to flip the card over

2) On the right, click the button titles 'To', and you will be prompted to type the name of the person you are giving the card to

3) Repeat step 2 for the 'Message' and 'From' buttons

4) Flip card over to see the final product. If you don't like the content you typed, you can just reclick the button to redo it
